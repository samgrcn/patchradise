Materials

Biodegradable by Design

Every single component of our patches is engineered to return safely to the earth and sea.

🧮

Patch Material

Made from plant-derived hydrocolloid that fully biodegrades within 180 days in marine environments. No synthetic polymers, no microplastics left behind.

💧

Adhesive Layer

Water-soluble adhesive that dissolves harmlessly in the ocean, leaving zero trace. Derived from natural plant-based compounds.

🌳

Backing Paper

Compostable release liner made from sustainably harvested wood pulp. Breaks down fully in home compost or commercial facilities.

🎨

Ink & Print

Soy-based inks on all packaging and patch markings. Non-toxic, biodegradable, and free from petroleum-derived chemicals.

Your Questions

Common Questions Answered

Transparency matters. Here are honest answers to the things people ask us most.

Q What happens if a patch falls off in the ocean?

It biodegrades completely within 180 days. No microplastics, no harmful residues. The materials break down into compounds naturally found in seawater. Your lost patch will not become pollution.

Q Is the packaging really plastic-free?

Yes, 100%. From the outer box to the inner pouch, every single component is either recycled, recyclable, or compostable. We audit every supplier to verify this claim, and we publish the results.

Q Do you offset your carbon emissions?

Yes. We partner with verified carbon offset programs and are actively working toward fully carbon-neutral operations by 2027. Every shipment is offset, and we publish an annual emissions report.

Q How do I dispose of used patches?

Used patches can go in your regular trash. They will biodegrade in standard landfill conditions. The backing paper is home-compostable, so if you have a compost bin, even better.

Q Do you test on animals?

Never. Patchradise is proudly cruelty-free and always will be. We use only synthetic and plant-based testing methods. No animal was harmed to make your surf session better.

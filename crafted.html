Quality Control

Five Steps to Perfection

Every single patch goes through a rigorous multi-step quality assurance process before it reaches your hands.

1

Raw Material Testing

Every incoming material batch is tested for purity, consistency, and performance before entering production.

2

Adhesion Testing

Patches are tested in saltwater immersion for 8+ hours to guarantee they hold through your longest sessions.

3

Skin Safety

Dermatological patch testing on sensitive skin panels ensures zero irritation and hypoallergenic performance.

4

Durability

Flex and stretch testing simulates real surf movements — paddling, duck diving, pop-ups — thousands of cycles.

5

Final Inspection

Visual and dimensional checks on every single patch before packaging. Nothing ships without passing inspection.

Materials & Sourcing

What Goes Into Every Patch

We're transparent about every material we use, where it comes from, and why we chose it.

Hydrocolloid Base

Medical-grade hydrocolloid sourced from certified suppliers. The same trusted material used in advanced wound care — gentle, effective, and proven.

Micro-Mesh Layer

Breathable polyurethane film produced in the USA. Allows air circulation while blocking friction — your skin stays cool and dry underneath.

Acrylic-based adhesive that is 100% latex-free and silicone-free. Designed for sensitive skin and tested to hold in saltwater for hours.

Common Questions

Answered, Openly

We get these questions a lot. Here are straight answers with nothing to hide.

"Is the facility FDA registered?"

Our manufacturing facility is FDA registered and ISO 13485 certified, meeting the highest medical-grade standards. Patchradise patches are classified as a skin barrier, not a drug, so individual FDA drug approval is not required — but we voluntarily hold ourselves to medical device manufacturing standards because that's the level of quality our surfers deserve.

"Where exactly are they made?"

Right here in California, just miles from the coast where we surf. We chose to manufacture locally so we can walk the production floor, catch problems in person, and get back in the water to test the same afternoon. No overseas factories, no long supply chains — just a tight loop between where we make them and where we use them.

"Are they tested on animals?"

Never. Patchradise is and always will be 100% cruelty-free. We test on willing surfers only. Our team, our friends, and our community of beta testers volunteer to put every prototype through real-world conditions. Animals should be in the ocean, not in a lab.

"What's the shelf life?"

Unopened patches last 24 months when stored in a cool, dry place. Each package is individually sealed to maintain adhesive integrity and sterility until the moment you peel it open and paddle out.

How to Use

Five Steps to a Perfect Session

A complete guide to getting the most out of every patch, from prep to storage.

💦

1. Prep Your Skin

Clean and thoroughly dry the skin area where you will apply the patch. Make sure there are no lotions, oils, or sunscreen on the surface. The adhesive bonds best to clean, dry skin for maximum hold throughout your session.

🧮

2. Apply the Patch

Peel the protective backing to expose the adhesive. Position the patch over your friction zone and press down firmly for a full 10 seconds. Run your finger along all the edges to smooth them flat against your skin, ensuring a secure, bubble-free seal.

🌊

3. Suit Up and Surf

Pull on your wetsuit normally. The patches are engineered to flex and move with your body. Their ultra-thin 0.3mm profile means zero bulk under your suit. Surf, paddle, duck dive, and wipe out without worry for up to 8 hours.

👍

4. Remove After Your Session

When your session is over, peel the patch slowly from one edge. Work gently across the surface to lift it without pulling on your skin. Rinse the area with fresh water. Warm water makes removal even smoother if you prefer.

📦

5. Store Your Remaining Patches

Place unused patches back in their resealable pouch and store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Proper storage keeps the adhesive fresh and effective for months, so your patches are always ready for your next session.

Common Questions

Everything Else You Want to Know

Straight answers to the questions surfers ask most about Patchradise.

🔄

Can I Reuse Them?

No. Patchradise patches are designed for single use only. After a session, the adhesive layer has absorbed moisture and contoured to your body. Reusing a patch means a weaker bond, less protection, and a higher risk of bacteria buildup. For optimal performance and hygiene, use a fresh patch each time you suit up.

🧥

Do They Work with Any Wetsuit?

Yes, absolutely. Patchradise patches are compatible with every wetsuit brand, thickness, and style on the market. Whether you are wearing a 2mm shorty in summer or a 6/5mm hooded suit in winter, the patches sit flush against your skin and perform identically. The neoprene simply glides over the smooth micro-mesh surface.

👏

Will They Leave Marks on My Skin?

No. The hypoallergenic adhesive is formulated to leave zero residue when removed. Most people experience no redness or marks at all. In rare cases, very sensitive skin may show slight pinkness that fades within minutes. If you have extremely reactive skin, try a small test patch on your forearm first.

✂️

Can I Trim Them to Fit?

Yes, you can. While the pre-cut shapes are designed to cover the most common friction zones perfectly, every body is different. Use clean scissors to trim any patch to your preferred size and shape. Just make sure the edges stay smooth after cutting so they seal flat against your skin.

☀️

Can I Wear Sunscreen with Them?

Yes, with one important step. Apply your sunscreen first and let it fully dry and absorb into your skin before placing the patch. If sunscreen is still wet or oily on the surface, the adhesive will not bond properly. Wait about five minutes after sunscreen application, then apply your patches to clean, dry skin.

🔢

How Many Do I Need Per Session?

Most surfers use two to three patches per session, depending on their personal friction zones. A typical setup is one patch on each side of the neck and one on the chest. If you also experience armpit chafing, add one or two more. Experiment with placement over a few sessions to find your ideal configuration.
